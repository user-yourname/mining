A game made in 18 hours by two people with no ideas on how comuters work.
Expect bugs and mess.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

WASD or arrow keys to move

press down to mine, the key you are holding when you mine is where you mine (you cannot mine up)

the blue bar is your stamina, if you run out of stamina, you cannot mine, go to house to regain your stamina

the yellow bar is how much gold you have on you, if it's full, you cannot gain more gain more gold, go to house to put the gold you have on you to the house, the number on the house is how much gold you have in the house (it's used to but upgrades)

there is one upgrade currently, more stamina (battery), this appears when you are at the house, the number on the upgrades is the cost

PRESS Q+L IF YOU GET STUCK /GO BACK TO HOUSE (you will lose all gold you have on you though, so be careful!)
